Don

Simple, booth-lined Japanese restaurant specializing in hot entrees like donburi, teriyaki & ramen! Don is a restaurant that's on the smaller side; however, the atmosphere, service, and some of the Korean and Japanese flavors that are present here hit the spot big time! The interior is cozy with Asian design elements, and it has a really comfortable vibe. The menu itself is also unique in that it offers mostly noodle and rice bowl dishes in a hot stone bowl!

Delicious, Private, Authentic. LOCATED ON 423 TATE STREET GREENSBORO Open Every day! 11:00 am- 10:30 pm
